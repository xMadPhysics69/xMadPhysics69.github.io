What makes this site lit af and unique from other sites?

Mad Physics was made intended for the teenage audience. This means that in all the study guides, crash courses, and explanation of each topic, modern day slang is used instead of your conventional textbook lingoo. However, other age ranges are free to utilize this site but may have a bit of a harder time getting a full grasp of the content.

But why slang?? Like you dumb af boii

Since freshmen year, I liked tutoring people. Whenever I made study guides using teen friendly language (slang), I found that it was easier for other students to understand math/science because they were able to relate to the language more. Whether or not you like math or science, you still need to pass your classes with good grades . However, textbooks mislead students into getting bored by math and science which then affects their ability to learn. I feel that with the use of more teen friendly language, it will be easier for people to learn more challenging material.
